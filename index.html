Uyare Learning


Uyare brings out the educated unemployed students of Muriyad GP to prepare in fulfilling their dream of a government job. Along with that, we help them to prepare for the higher education levels such as Civil Service. We help and prepare the students to be both physically and mentally strong to have a bright future in their carrier.
As a second step, we are aiming to extend care to school-level students for their bright future and to make them aware of time management, personality development, physical fitness, mental balancing, goal setting, and carrier planning.

Easy Learning Platform

Aspirants can always listen to lectures as whatsapp audios even while they work, play or eat as we use, adaptable learning platforms like WhatsApp & Telegram from where they can easily access the learning modules.

One to One Mentorship

Do you miss that serious push or care from your teachers or parents that motivated you? In Uyare Learning every candidate is provided with a one to one mentorship throughout the learning process to keep them motivated.
